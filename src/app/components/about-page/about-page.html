About Premios Miscelánea

Premios Miscelánea is a platform dedicated to celebrating and recognizing the best in the world of gaming. Our mission is to provide gamers with a space to discover, vote for, and celebrate their favorite games across various categories.

Our Story

Founded in 2026, Premios Miscelánea was born out of a passion for gaming and a desire to create a community-driven awards platform. We believe that gamers should have a voice in recognizing the incredible work of game developers and the impact games have on our lives.

How It Works

Each year, we curate a list of nominated games across multiple categories, including Best Game, Best Art Direction, Best Storytelling, and more. Gamers can then vote for their favorites, and the winners are announced at our annual awards ceremony.

Join Us

Whether you're a casual gamer or a hardcore enthusiast, we invite you to join our community and be part of the celebration. Follow us on social media, participate in our voting process, and help us honor the best in gaming!
